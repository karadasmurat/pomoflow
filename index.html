The loop

Four steps.
At your pace.

Progress isn't built in marathons. It's built in honest, repeated acts of showing up. Small yeses, repeated daily, compound into real progress.

01 — Define
Name what matters

Define what deserves your attention. Add the areas of work, study, or life you want to invest in and give each a color. These aren't just labels — they're a declaration of what you're choosing to show up for.

02 — Commit
Set your intent

Before the timer starts, name where this block of time is going. That single conscious choice — made before the work begins — is where busy becomes purposeful.

03 — Focus
Show up fully

Work. Rest. Repeat. Your only job is to be present for the session you committed to. Progress is built in honest, repeated acts of showing up.

04 — Reflect
See where you went

Review how your effort is flowing across focus areas — not to judge, but to understand. One honest look is worth more than a week of good intentions.
