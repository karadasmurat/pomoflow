Focus on your flow

PomoFlow is a minimalist productivity tool designed to help you maintain deep focus and track your goals with precision. Built on the core principles of the Pomodoro technique, it combines intuitive design with powerful analytics.

How it works

  1. Set your goal

    Add a new goal to your list and give it a distinct color to help you categorize your work.

  2. Start your flow

    Choose a goal you want to work on. Click play to start a 25-minute focus session.

  3. Rest and recharge

    After each focus session, take a short break. After four sessions, take a longer break to stay fresh.

  4. Analyze progress

    Review your session history and goal breakdown to see where you're most productive.

The PomoFlow Philosophy

Propel your productivity with uninterrupted focus sessions. Link goals directly to the timer to see exactly where your time goes.

Your data stays with you. PomoFlow uses local storage to keep your goals and history secure on your own device, never on a remote server.

Keyboard Shortcuts

SpaceStart or pause the timer.
RReset the current session.
NSkip to the next session.
GToggle the goal drawer open or closed.
1Switch to Focus mode.
2Switch to Short Break.
3Switch to Long Break.
