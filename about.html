About & How to Use

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into focused intervals, separated by short breaks. This trains your brain to focus and helps prevent burnout.

  1. Choose a task you want to work on.
  2. Set the timer for 25 minutes and start working without interruption.
  3. When the timer rings, take a 5-minute short break.
  4. After four focus sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break.

The PomoFlow Lifecycle

PomoFlow automates this cycle for you. The visual Session Progress dots inside the timer show your position in the cycle. As you complete each focus session, the dots track your journey toward a long break.
