Intentional Focus

PomoFlow is a minimalist productivity tool designed to help you maintain deep focus and track your goals with precision.

The PomoFlow Philosophy

From "being busy" to making meaningful progress.

Productivity begins with intent. By attributing every focus block to a specific goal, you ensure that every minute counts toward what truly matters.

PomoFlow provides the clarity you need to stay aware of your effort and intentional with your time—helping you stop wondering where the day went and start seeing exactly where your energy is going.

YOUR DATA IS YOURS. PomoFlow uses local storage to keep your goals and history secure on your own device.

How it works

Define your Goals

Add the goals you want to focus on. Give them distinct colors to visually categorize your effort.

Set your Intent

Link your session to a goal using the [+ Goal] button to ensure every minute counts.

Focus and Flow

Start your session. Get dynamic guidance to transition smoothly between focus and rest.

Analyze and Adapt

Review your breakdown. With 4 AM rollovers, your night sessions stay with the day they started.

Choose your Persona

PomoFlow isn't just a tool; it's a companion. Choose an avatar that matches your current mindset or the specific energy you need for your goals.
